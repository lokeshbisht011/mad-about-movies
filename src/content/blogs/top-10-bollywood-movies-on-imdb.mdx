# Top 10 Bollywood Movies on IMDb

Bollywood has produced numerous masterpieces over the years. Here's a list of the top 10 Bollywood movies as rated on IMDb:

## 1. Sholay (1975)
![Sholay](/images/movie1.jpg)

*IMDb Rating: 8.2/10*

*Sholay* is a classic tale of friendship and revenge, featuring Amitabh Bachchan, Dharmendra, and Amjad Khan. Directed by Ramesh Sippy, this movie is a landmark in Indian cinema.

## 2. Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge (1995)
![Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge](/images/movie2.jpg)

*IMDb Rating: 8.1/10*

*Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge*, also known as DDLJ, is a timeless love story starring Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol. Directed by Aditya Chopra, this film has been running in theaters since its release.

## 3. Lagaan (2001)
![Lagaan](/images/movie3.jpg)

*IMDb Rating: 8.1/10*

*Lagaan* is a period drama directed by Ashutosh Gowariker, starring Aamir Khan. The story revolves around a cricket match between British officers and Indian villagers.

## 4. 3 Idiots (2009)
![3 Idiots](/images/movie4.jpg)

*IMDb Rating: 8.4/10*

Directed by Rajkumar Hirani, *3 Idiots* is a comedy-drama about the lives of three engineering students. The film stars Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, and Sharman Joshi.

## 5. Taare Zameen Par (2007)
![Taare Zameen Par](/images/movie5.jpg)

*IMDb Rating: 8.4/10*

*Taare Zameen Par* is a heartwarming story about a dyslexic child and his art teacher, starring Darsheel Safary and Aamir Khan, who also directed the film.

## 6. Gangs of Wasseypur (2012)
![Gangs of Wasseypur](/images/movie6.jpg)

*IMDb Rating: 8.2/10*

Directed by Anurag Kashyap, *Gangs of Wasseypur* is an epic crime film that explores the coal mafia of Dhanbad and the power struggles, politics, and vengeance between three crime families.

## 7. Black (2005)
![Black](/images/movie7.jpg)

*IMDb Rating: 8.2/10*

*Black* is a drama film directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali, starring Amitabh Bachchan and Rani Mukerji. It tells the story of a blind and deaf girl and her relationship with her teacher.

## 8. Pather Panchali (1955)
![Pather Panchali](/images/movie8.jpg)

*IMDb Rating: 8.5/10*

Directed by Satyajit Ray, *Pather Panchali* is a Bengali drama film and the first part of the Apu Trilogy. It depicts the life of a poor family in rural Bengal.

## 9. Swades (2004)
![Swades](/images/movie9.jpg)

*IMDb Rating: 8.2/10*

*Swades* is a drama film directed by Ashutosh Gowariker, starring Shah Rukh Khan. The story follows an NRI who returns to India to take his nanny to the United States, but ends up rediscovering his roots.

## 10. Andhadhun (2018)
![Andhadhun](/images/movie10.jpg)

*IMDb Rating: 8.2/10*

*Andhadhun* is a black comedy crime thriller directed by Sriram Raghavan, starring Ayushmann Khurrana, Tabu, and Radhika Apte. The film revolves around a blind pianist who unwittingly becomes embroiled in a series of murders.

These movies have left an indelible mark on Bollywood and continue to be loved by audiences worldwide.
