# Top 10 Bollywood Movies on IMDb in 2023

The year 2023 has been a fantastic one for Bollywood, with a plethora of movies that have not only captivated audiences but also garnered high ratings on IMDb. Here is a list of the top 10 Bollywood movies on IMDb in 2023.

## 1. **Pathaan**

**Rating**: 8.5/10

**Summary**: Shah Rukh Khan stars in this action-packed thriller that keeps you on the edge of your seat. With breathtaking stunts and an engaging storyline, *Pathaan* is a must-watch.

![Pathaan](/images/pathaan.jpg)

## 2. **Jawan**

**Rating**: 8.4/10

**Summary**: Another blockbuster featuring Shah Rukh Khan, *Jawan* is a gripping tale of patriotism and heroism.

![Jawan](/images/jawan.jpg)

## 3. **Rocky Aur Rani Ki Prem Kahani**

**Rating**: 8.3/10

**Summary**: A romantic drama starring Ranveer Singh and Alia Bhatt, this film explores the complexities of modern relationships.

![Rocky Aur Rani Ki Prem Kahani](/images/rocky-aur-rani.jpg)

## 4. **Brahmastra Part One: Shiva**

**Rating**: 8.2/10

**Summary**: This fantasy adventure film featuring Ranbir Kapoor and Alia Bhatt takes you on a mystical journey.

![Brahmastra](/images/brahmastra.jpg)

## 5. **Tiger 3**

**Rating**: 8.1/10

**Summary**: Salman Khan returns in this high-octane action movie that promises thrilling sequences and a compelling plot.

![Tiger 3](/images/tiger3.jpg)

## 6. **Dunki**

**Rating**: 8.0/10

**Summary**: Directed by Rajkumar Hirani, *Dunki* is a thought-provoking film that addresses societal issues with a touch of humor.

![Dunki](/images/dunki.jpg)

## 7. **Bheed**

**Rating**: 7.9/10

**Summary**: A powerful drama that delves into the struggles faced by the common man during a crisis.

![Bheed](/images/bheed.jpg)

## 8. **Sam Bahadur**

**Rating**: 7.8/10

**Summary**: This biographical drama about Field Marshal Sam Manekshaw stars Vicky Kaushal in the titular role.

![Sam Bahadur](/images/sam-bahadur.jpg)

## 9. **Pippa**

**Rating**: 7.7/10

**Summary**: A war drama that showcases the bravery and sacrifices of soldiers during the Indo-Pak war.

![Pippa](/images/pippa.jpg)

## 10. **Bawaal**

**Rating**: 7.6/10

**Summary**: A romantic drama featuring Varun Dhawan and Janhvi Kapoor, *Bawaal* is a heartwarming tale of love and loss.

![Bawaal](/images/bawaal.jpg)
