# Arrange The Scenes - A Bollywood Puzzle Game

Bollywood movies often have intricate storylines with scenes that leave a lasting impression. *Arrange The Scenes* is a game that captures this essence by challenging players to put movie scenes in the correct order. 

## How to Play

In this game, you are presented with a series of scenes from a Bollywood movie. Your task is to rearrange them in the correct sequence to recreate the storyline. It's a test of your memory and understanding of Bollywood plots.

## Why Play Arrange The Scenes?

- **Challenge Your Memory**: Recall and order scenes from your favorite movies.
- **Interactive Fun**: Engage with Bollywood movies in a new and exciting way.
- **Compete with Friends**: See who can arrange the scenes the fastest and most accurately.
