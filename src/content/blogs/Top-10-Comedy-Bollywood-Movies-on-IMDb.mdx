# Top 10 Comedy Bollywood Movies on IMDb

Bollywood has a rich history of comedy films that have made audiences laugh for decades. Here is a list of the top 10 comedy Bollywood movies on IMDb.

## 1. **3 Idiots**

**Rating**: 8.4/10

**Summary**: A hilarious yet insightful movie about the pressure of education in India, starring Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, and Sharman Joshi. This film beautifully balances humor and a strong social message.

![3 Idiots](/images/3-idiots.jpg)

## 2. **Hera Pheri**

**Rating**: 8.1/10

**Summary**: A classic comedy about three men who get entangled in a kidnapping plot. Starring Akshay Kumar, Sunil Shetty, and Paresh Rawal, *Hera Pheri* is a laugh riot from start to finish.

![Hera Pheri](/images/hera-pheri.jpg)

## 3. **Munna Bhai M.B.B.S.**

**Rating**: 8.1/10

**Summary**: Sanjay Dutt stars as a gangster who tries to fulfill his fatherâ€™s dream by becoming a doctor, leading to a series of comedic and heartwarming events.

![Munna Bhai M.B.B.S.](/images/munna-bhai.jpg)

## 4. **Chupke Chupke**

**Rating**: 8.0/10

**Summary**: A comedy of errors about a botany professor who plays a prank on his in-laws. This 1975 classic starring Dharmendra, Amitabh Bachchan, and Sharmila Tagore remains a favorite.

![Chupke Chupke](/images/chupke-chupke.jpg)

## 5. **Andaz Apna Apna**

**Rating**: 8.0/10

**Summary**: A cult favorite, this film features Aamir Khan and Salman Khan as two slackers who compete to win the heart of a rich heiress, leading to endless comedy.

![Andaz Apna Apna](/images/andaz-apna-apna.jpg)

## 6. **Golmaal: Fun Unlimited**

**Rating**: 7.7/10

**Summary**: A comedy about four friends who lie to get a place to stay and get caught up in a series of humorous situations. Directed by Rohit Shetty, this film is the beginning of the popular *Golmaal* series.

![Golmaal: Fun Unlimited](/images/golmaal.jpg)

## 7. **Padosan**

**Rating**: 7.6/10

**Summary**: A hilarious musical comedy about a simpleton who falls in love with his beautiful neighbor and tries to win her heart with the help of his friends. Featuring Sunil Dutt, Saira Banu, and Kishore Kumar.

![Padosan](/images/padosan.jpg)

## 8. **Jaane Bhi Do Yaaro**

**Rating**: 8.4/10

**Summary**: This satirical comedy takes a look at the dark side of bureaucracy and corruption in India. The film follows two photographers who accidentally capture a murder on film. 

![Jaane Bhi Do Yaaro](/images/jaane-bhi-do-yaaro.jpg)

## 9. **Dhamaal**

**Rating**: 7.4/10

**Summary**: A modern comedy about four lazy friends who discover a hidden treasure and embark on a wild goose chase to find it, leading to a series of funny situations. 

![Dhamaal](/images/dhamaal.jpg)

## 10. **Welcome**

**Rating**: 7.0/10

**Summary**: A hilarious film featuring Akshay Kumar, Katrina Kaif, Anil Kapoor, and Nana Patekar about a man trying to marry into a crime family without getting involved in their business.

![Welcome](/images/welcome.jpg)
